Delete Your MoveThink Account

MoveThink is a walking companion for thinking clearly. We respect your right to delete your account and all associated data at any time.

Option 1: Delete in the app (fastest)

  1. Open MoveThink on your device
  2. Tap the Profile tab at the bottom
  3. Scroll to the bottom of the screen to the danger zone
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Type DELETE in the confirmation field
  6. Tap the destructive Delete button to confirm

Your account and all associated data will be removed immediately.

Option 2: Request deletion by email

If you no longer have access to the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us:

Email: dmshuklaa@gmail.com
Subject line: MoveThink Account Deletion Request
Include: The email address associated with your MoveThink account

We will process your request within 7 business days and confirm by email when complete.

What gets deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your account profile (name, email, user ID)
  • All session records (topics, durations, ratings, takeaways)
  • All text notes captured during sessions
  • All voice recordings stored on your device
  • Reminder settings and routine preferences

What is retained

We do not retain user data after account deletion. Sign-in records held by Apple or Google (independent of MoveThink) are not within our control — to manage those, visit your Apple ID settings or Google account settings.

Questions?

If you have any questions about account deletion or data handling, contact us at dmshuklaa@gmail.com.
